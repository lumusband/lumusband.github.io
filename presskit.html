Short Bio

Lumus is a 5-piece cover band from Farmington, Maine: Multi-decade covers (’70s to now) with a sweet spot in the late ’90s and 2000s. Tight grooves, big vocals, and harmonies that land—then we keep the energy up until the room turns into a dance floor.

What’s Included in a Press Kit

Essential

  • Short + long band bio
  • High-res promo photos (landscape + portrait)
  • Logo (PNG + SVG if possible)
  • Links: website, socials, Spotify, video
  • Contact + booking info

For venues / production

  • Stage plot
  • Input list
  • Tech rider (power, PA needs, monitors)

Nice-to-have

  • Testimonials / quotes
  • Sample setlist
  • Performance video links
  • One-sheet PDF
