Short Bio

Lumus is a five-piece cover band out of Farmington, Maine, serving up rock-pop anthems from the ’70s to right about now—always chasing the “oh-hellz-yeah!” moment when the chorus hits and the whole room comes alive. We hand-pick every song (no filler), rehearse it tight, and deliver it with big harmonies, punchy grooves, and high energy.

Press Release Bio

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Farmington, Maine — Born from a chance meeting over a red drum kit in a pawnshop window, Lumus is a five-piece live cover band dedicated to the “oh-hellz-yeah!” moment—the instant a familiar hook lands and the crowd turns into a choir. Lumus pulls from rock-pop classics and modern anthems (the ’70s to right about now), choosing songs for one reason: they move a room.

Instead of chasing trends or padding sets with filler, Lumus hand-picks every track and works it until it’s tight: punchy rhythm section, bright guitar, and big, confident vocals. The result is a set that feels like a party playlist—only louder, live, and with the kind of energy that gets people off chairs and onto the floor.

Since forming in 2022, Lumus has built a following across central and western Maine, bringing the party to venues such as Homestead Kitchen Bar & Bakery, Farmhouse Beer Garden, and Ayuh Cider. The band is available for clubs, festivals, private events, and weddings, with flexible set lengths and professional sound support.

“This band is like wait-a-minute—What? Hellz yeah!” —Shirley, Skowhegan
